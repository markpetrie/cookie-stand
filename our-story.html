A love for salmon, coffee and cookies


Pat grew up fishing with his grandfather on Puget Sound. Every morning his grandfather would pour Pat a cup of steaming hot coffee and hand him one if his grandmother's fresh baked salmon cookies. When Pat graduated college, he opened his first coffee shop near Pike Street Market. Every morning he would bake himself a batch of his grandma's salmon cookies while opening the coffee shop. One morning a customer asked what smelled so good and Pat offered him a cookie. He love it! Pat added his salmon cookies to the menu the next day and the rest is history.
